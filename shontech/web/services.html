Oilfield Manpower Supply

Shontech is a household name in supply of eminently qualified technical and non-technical personnel for the oil and gas industry. Our manpower supply capabilities range from crewing of rigs, offshore vessels, and floating production units/platforms, to staffing of onshore flowstations and process facilities. We have a robust database of highly qualified Oil & Gas personnel, including production operators, E&I specialists, static & rotating equipment experts, marine crew, QA/QC personnel, project management staff, and engineers; having the requisite field-based experience to fulfil critical Oil & Gas project tasks. Our commitment is to provide our clients the most spec-fit personnel for any oilfield task, allowing enhanced operations/project delivery, at the most efficient resource utilization.

Oil & Gas Insurance

All aspects of oil and gas business is laden with risk. From geological uncertainty, and hazardous work environments to sudden changes in oil price, risk pervades the oil and gas landscape from project initiation to close-out. To mitigate the adverse effects of risks, oil & gas operators employ various kinds of insurance policies, ranging from construction all risk insurance, marine insurance, goods in transit insurance, hull & machinery insurance, collision risk insurance, personnel and indemnity insurance, war risk insurance, amongst others. Whatever your risk and insurance needs, Shontech Projects Limited works with a trusted syndicate of local & international insurance underwriters to provide the required cover.

Oilfield Logistics

Oil Exploration and Production activities (onshore & offshore) often involve continuous movement of large amounts of goods, personnel and services from place of manufacture/origin to point of utilization. Whether you are importing specialized equipment from overseas, transporting pre-fabricated materials offshore, or delivering crew and consumables to a remote location, Shontech Projects is your go-to partner for reliable, safe and on-time transportation of oilfield goods and services. We manage a large fleet of IOC pre-mobbed logistics equipment, including barges, supply vessels, cranes, load beds, and work boats; guaranteeing best-in class logistics service for both onshore and offshore operations.
